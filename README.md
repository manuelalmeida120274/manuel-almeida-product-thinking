# From Developer to AI-Oriented Product Manager

A short collection of articles about ERP, product management and the evolution of business software, written from the perspective of someone who moved from software development and functional analysis into product strategy, ERP innovation and AI-oriented product management.

---

## About

I am Manuel Almeida, a Product Manager with 25+ years of experience in ERP, software development, functional analysis and product innovation.

My work sits at the intersection of business software, product strategy and technology. I am especially interested in how ERP and business platforms evolve with AI, intelligent automation, contextual analytics, agent-oriented experiences and better product judgment.

This page brings together a few articles that reflect how I think about useful software, AI-native business products and the evolution from developer to product manager.
